Flexible Body Sensors

Category : Electrical Engineering
Class : Research
Completion : June 2019
Role : reasearcher

I worked on creating a novel manufacturing method of flexible printed circuit boards. These were boards were constructed from a polymer that has self healing properties, allow itself to fuse back together under heat. Many different models were designed including, flex sensor for tracking chewing, accelerometer for tracking movement, and an electromyography for measuring muscle movement. The boards were modular ,and could be daisy chained together to create bundle of sensors.
